Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy tickets?

Browse our events page, select an event, choose your ticket type and quantity, and proceed to checkout. Payments are securely processed via MTN Mobile Money.

How do I host an event?

Visit our "Host Event" page, fill out the event details form, and submit. Our team will review your submission, and once approved, your event will be live on GentsConcerts.

What payment methods are accepted?

Currently, we accept payments exclusively through MTN Mobile Money for all ticket purchases.

How do I get my payout as a host?

Host payouts are processed within 3 to 5 business days after your event date via MTN Mobile Money to the registered organizer number provided during event submission.

What is the commission structure?

For the first 2 months from launch, we charge zero commission. After this promotional period, commissions range from 8% to 15% depending on the ticket price. Free events always have zero commission. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions for full details.

Are there refunds?

All ticket sales are final unless an event is cancelled by the organizer. In case of cancellation, full refunds are issued within 7 business days. Please see our Terms and Conditions for more information.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team via email at gentsconcerts@gmail.com or through our Facebook page: GentsConcerts Facebook.

What is the launch date?

The official launch date for GentsConcerts is August 1, 2026.
