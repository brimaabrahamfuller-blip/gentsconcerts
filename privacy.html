Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

GentsConcerts is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website gentsconcerts.netlify.app, including any other media form, media channel, mobile website, or mobile application related or connected thereto (collectively, the “Site”). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Site.

2. Data Collection

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Site, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when you participate in activities on the Site, or otherwise when you contact us. The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the Site, the choices you make and the products and features you use. The personal information we collect can include the following:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and other similar contact data.
  • Financial Data: Data necessary to process your payment if you make purchases, such as your MTN Mobile Money details. All payment data is stored by MTN Mobile Money. We do not store your full payment card numbers.
  • User Account Data: Information related to your account, such as event listings, ticket sales, and purchase history.

3. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Site to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Process your transactions and send you related information, including purchase confirmations and invoices.
  • Enable user-to-user communications.
  • Generate a personal profile about you to make your future visits to the Site more personalized.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the Site.
  • Notify you of updates to the Site or our services.

4. MTN Mobile Money Transaction Data Handling

When you use MTN Mobile Money for transactions on GentsConcerts, your transaction data is handled directly by MTN Mobile Money. We receive confirmation of payment but do not store sensitive financial details. We ensure that all integrations with MTN Mobile Money adhere to their security protocols and privacy standards.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, and other tracking technologies on the Site to help customize the Site and improve your experience. When you access the Site, your personal information is not collected through the use of tracking technology. Most browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can remove or reject cookies, but be aware that such action could affect the availability and functionality of the Site.

6. Third-Party Sharing Policy

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information unless we provide users with advance notice. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release information when its release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property or safety.

7. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal information. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at gentsconcerts@gmail.com.

8. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of Liberia.
