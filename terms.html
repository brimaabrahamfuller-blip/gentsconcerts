Terms and Conditions

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to GentsConcerts, Liberia's premier event ticketing platform, proudly operated from Monrovia, Liberia. These Terms and Conditions govern your use of our services.

2. Introductory Offer

For the first 2 months from the date of platform launch, GentsConcerts will charge zero commission on all ticket sales. Event hosts and organizers may list and sell tickets completely free during this promotional period. This offer is subject to change with 14 days written notice via email.

3. Post-Promotional Commission Structure

Following the introductory offer, the following tiered commission structure will apply:

Ticket Price (USD) Commission per Ticket Sold
$1 to $10 8%
$11 to $25 10%
$26 to $50 12%
Above $50 15%
Free Events 0% (always zero commission)
VIP and VVIP Tiers Same commission structure based on ticket price

4. Payment and Payout

Commissions are deducted automatically at the point of sale. Host payouts are processed within 3 to 5 business days after the event date via MTN Mobile Money to the registered organizer number.

5. Ticket Refund Policy

All ticket sales are final unless the event is cancelled by the organizer. In case of cancellation, full refunds are issued within 7 business days.

6. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining accurate account information, adhering to prohibited conduct guidelines, and respecting intellectual property rights.

7. Limitation of Liability

GentsConcerts is protected from third-party disputes between hosts and attendees.

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Liberia.

9. Contact

For any questions regarding these terms, please contact us at gentsconcerts@gmail.com or visit our Facebook page: GentsConcerts Facebook.
